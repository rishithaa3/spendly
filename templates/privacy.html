Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history and summaries.
  • To identify and fix bugs or usability issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before being saved. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typefaces. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. You can also review our Terms and Conditions.
